Policy ID: POL-019
Title: Remote Work Policy
Version: 3.0
Effective Date: 2025-01-01

Section 1: Purpose
This policy outlines the rules and expectations regarding telecommuting requirements, home office standards, and collaboration guidelines across all business units.

Section 2: Scope
This applies to employees, contractors, and authorized third parties interacting with company systems or information.

Section 3: Key Requirements
• Passwords must be at least 12 characters long.
• All violations must be reported within 24 hours.
• Records must be retained for a minimum of 3 years.
• Gifts above $150 are prohibited.
• Standard working hours are 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM, unless otherwise approved.

Section 4: Enforcement
Non‑compliance may result in disciplinary action, access revocation, or legal escalation.

Section 5: Contacts
Questions about this policy should be directed to compliance@company.com.
